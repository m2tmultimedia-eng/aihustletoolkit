💰 Side Hustle Profit Calculator

Estimate your monthly and yearly profit based on your work hours, hourly rate, and expenses.

How this works

This calculator multiplies your weekly hours by your hourly rate, estimates your monthly income, then subtracts your monthly expenses to show your projected profit.

Important note

This is a simple planning estimate. It does not include taxes, platform fees, refunds, inconsistent work, or unexpected business costs.

Example

If you work 10 hours per week at $25 per hour, that is about $1,000 per month before expenses. If your expenses are $150, your estimated monthly profit would be $850.

🚀 Want to build real income?

Use this number as your starting point, then improve your pricing, reduce expenses, or increase your weekly hours to grow your profit.
