💰 Side Hustle Profit Calculator

Estimate how much profit your side hustle could make each month and year.

How this works

This tool multiplies your weekly hours by your hourly rate, estimates monthly income, and subtracts your monthly expenses to show your projected profit.

Why it matters

A lot of people start hustles off vibes alone. Respectfully, vibes do not pay bills. This helps you see whether the numbers actually make sense.

Example

If you work 10 hours per week at $25 per hour, that’s about $1,000 per month before expenses. If your monthly expenses are $150, your estimated monthly profit would be $850.
