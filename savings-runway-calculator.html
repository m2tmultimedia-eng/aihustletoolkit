🏦 Savings Runway Calculator

See how many months your savings can cover your expenses.

How this works

This calculator divides your total savings by your monthly expenses to estimate how many months of runway you currently have.

Important note

This assumes your expenses stay fairly consistent. It does not account for emergencies, surprise bills, debt payments, or new income coming in while your savings are being used.

Example

If you have $6,000 saved and your monthly expenses are $2,000, then your current runway is about 3 months.

🚀 Why this matters

Runway buys breathing room. The more of it you have, the less likely you are to make panic decisions with your money.
