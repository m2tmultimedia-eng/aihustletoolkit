🏦 Savings Runway Calculator

How this works

This calculator divides your total savings by your monthly expenses to estimate how many months you can sustain your current lifestyle without new income.

Important note

This assumes your expenses stay consistent. It does not account for emergencies, unexpected costs, or income changes. Always aim for a buffer beyond the minimum.

Example

If you have $6,000 saved and your monthly expenses are $2,000, you have approximately 3 months of financial runway.

🚀 Why this matters

Runway is your safety net. The more months you have, the more freedom you gain to take risks, grow your hustle, and make smarter decisions.
