🤔 Should I Quit My Job Calculator

Compare your side hustle income against your monthly expenses.

How this works

This calculator compares your monthly side hustle income with your monthly expenses to see whether your income currently covers your base financial needs.

Important note

This is not financial advice. Savings, debt, health insurance, taxes, emergency funds, and job stability should all be considered before making a major decision.

Example

If your side hustle earns $3,500 per month and your expenses are $2,800, your income covers your expenses. If your income is $1,500 and expenses are $2,400, you may need more runway first.
