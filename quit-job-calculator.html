🤔 Should I Quit My Job? Calculator

Compare your side hustle income against your monthly expenses.

How this works

This tool compares your current monthly side hustle income with your monthly expenses to see if your income covers your base financial needs.

Important note

This is a basic financial reality check, not life advice carved in stone. Savings, health insurance, debt, emergencies, and job stability still matter.

Example

If your side hustle brings in $3,500 per month and your monthly expenses are $2,800, you may be in a stronger position than someone earning $1,500 with $2,400 in expenses.
