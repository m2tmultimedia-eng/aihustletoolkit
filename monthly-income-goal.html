📈 Monthly Income Goal Calculator

Estimate how much monthly income you can generate based on your hours and rate.

How this works

This calculator multiplies your weekly hours by your hourly rate, then estimates your monthly income based on a 4-week month.

Important note

This is a simple estimate. It does not include taxes, missed work, cancellations, refunds, or uneven income from month to month.

Example

If you work 15 hours per week at $25 per hour, your estimated monthly income would be about $1,500.
