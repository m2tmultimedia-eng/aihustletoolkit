📈 Monthly Income Calculator

How this works

This calculator multiplies your weekly working hours by your hourly rate, then estimates your total monthly income based on a 4-week month.

Important note

This is a basic estimate. It doesn’t include taxes, inconsistent work, or unexpected downtime. Real income can fluctuate, especially when you're just starting out.

Example

If you work 15 hours per week at $25 per hour, your estimated monthly income would be around $1,500.

🚀 Want to build real income?

Use this as your baseline, then increase your rate or hours strategically to grow faster.
